# AssetMind AI — Project Brief

## PS 8 Summary

**ET AI Hackathon 2026 — PS 8: AI for Industrial Knowledge Intelligence (Unified Asset
& Operations Brain).**

PS 8 calls for an AI-powered Industrial Knowledge Intelligence platform that ingests
heterogeneous industrial documents — engineering drawings, maintenance records, safety
procedures, inspection reports, operating instructions, project files, PDFs,
spreadsheets, scanned forms, and email archives. The platform must make this knowledge
**queryable, actionable, and continuously updated**.

## Final Product Goal

Build an **asset-centric industrial knowledge intelligence platform** — an "operations
brain" organized around physical assets, that turns scattered industrial documents into
unified, citation-backed, continuously updated knowledge.

## Core Modules Planned

- **Document ingestion** — ingest heterogeneous industrial documents and extract text.
- **RAG copilot** — citation-backed retrieval-augmented question answering over the
  knowledge base.
- **Asset knowledge graph** — link documents, assets, and operations into a connected
  graph.
- **RCA agent** — maintenance and root-cause-analysis assistance.
- **Compliance gap agent** — detect compliance and safety procedure gaps.
- **Benchmark dashboard** — evaluation and benchmarking of system performance.

## Week 1 Goal

Establish the RAG foundation:

- Upload documents.
- Extract text.
- Store document chunks.
- Stand up a basic **citation-backed RAG** pipeline.

## Current Stage

**Repository bootstrap.** Project context files are being established. No frontend or
backend has been built yet.
